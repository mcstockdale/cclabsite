Bombardier R142

Built by from 1993 to 2003 in Quebec, Vermont, and Plattsburgh, New York.
1030 cars were built total.
They are 51 feet long, 8.5 feet wide, and 12 feet tall.
There are 3 doors on each side.
Their top speed is 66 miles per hour.
A 5 car train has an equivalent of 2065 horsepower from its electric motors
They run on the 2, 4, and 5 lines.
These were the first trains to feature "new technology." They had modern features such as automated station announcements, LED information screens, and more space overall.
There is a model similar in appearance to this one, the R188.
