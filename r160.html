Alstom R160

Built by from 2006 to 2010 in Germany, Japan, Nebraska, and Upstate New York.
1662 cars were built total.
They are 60 feet long, 10 feet wide, and 12 feet tall.
There are 4 doors on each side.
Their top speed is 55 miles per hour.
The electric motors make an equivalent of 150 horsepower. Which makes 2400 horsepower on a 4 car train.
They run on the J, Z, L, E, F, G, and R lines.
These were one of the first trains to feature "new technology." They had modern features such as automated station announcements, LED information screens, and more space overall.
These are an upgraded version of the R143.
