Kawasaki R188

Built by from 2010 to 2016 in Japan and Upstate New York, the first ones entered service in 2013.
506 cars were built total.
They are 51 feet long, 8.5 feet wide, and 12 feet tall.
There are 3 doors on each side.
Their top speed is 70 miles per hour.
A 5 car train has an equivalent of 2100 horsepower from its electric motors
They run on the 7 line exclusively.
This model is an upgraded version of the R142. It features technology for automatic train operation.
The trains can drive themselves to an extent. They know their exact position on the tracks in relation to other trains.
This technology is called Communications Based Train Control, or CBTC.
