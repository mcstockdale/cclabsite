Pullman Standard R46

Built from 1975 to 1978 in Chicago.
754 cars total were delivered.
They are 75 feet long, 10 feet wide, and 12 feet tall.
There are 4 doors on each side.
Their top speed is 55 miles per hour.
Their motors make an equivalent of 115 horsepower.
This model is one of the oldest type that exists in the system. They are slow, clunky, and hard to maintain because of their age.
