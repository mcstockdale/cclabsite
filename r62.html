Kawasaki R62

Built from 1983 to 1985 in Japan.
325 cars were built total.
They are 51 feet long, 8.5 feet wide, and 12 feet tall.
There are 3 doors on each side.
Their top speed is 55 miles per hour.
Their motors make an equivalent of 115 horsepower, they are built by Bombardier.
They run on the 3 line.
There is an almost identical model, the R62A. This alternate model is built by Bombardier.
